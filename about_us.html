About Us

Hi, we are Geosight!

What is Geosight?

Geosight is a website that showcases the budget-friendly hidden gems of countries all over Europe. Discover per country, see our top picks, or explore our interactive map of Europe - you choose the path towards your next adventure!

Our Mission

What we strive for with Geosight is affordable travel that delivers unique experiences catered to students. With this project, we hope to provide inspiration and bring attention to trip destinations that young people will treasure and look back to fondly.

Our Team

Hello! We are Martina, Vini, Lucas and Yavor, four ICT & Media Design students, passionate about travel and going off the beaten track. Combining our diverse skills and shared interest led to what you now see and know as Geosight!
